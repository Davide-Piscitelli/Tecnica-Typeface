04 // INFORMATION & SPECIFICATIONS

Tecnica 55 and Tecnica 53 are digital writing styles built entirely on structured geometric pixel grids. The project draws clear visual inspiration from the legacy of early technological devices, capturing the precise aesthetic of early computing terminal systems, low-resolution screen displays, and historical dot matrix printers.

Built upon robust matrix frameworks, both font systems seamlessly anchor visual consistency, computational balance, and interface clarity.

The structured architectural nature of the grid renders clean geometry optimized for digital interfaces, technical readouts, and developer console visualizer nodes.
